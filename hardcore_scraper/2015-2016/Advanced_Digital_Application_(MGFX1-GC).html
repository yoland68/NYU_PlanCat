This course is dedicated to all aspects of pre-production planning, project management and presentation. Students will learn the technical fundamentals of computer graphics as they apply to a professional production pipeline. Focus areas of this course will include: concept development, research, bidding, budgeting / finance, production design, and technical testing. The goal of this course is to develop a full treatment and project plan as used in various professional design studios to communicate a visual treatment to clients and audiences from a variety of backgrounds. Students will make highly effective decisions that effect many areas of digital production for media projects.
